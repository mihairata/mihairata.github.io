Trio

Trio is a Galaga inspired 2D shooter. I added my own twist on the orginal formula and added a form switching mechanic. The player must cycle through different versions of the main character based on which enemy is on the screen. It works like rock-paper-scizzors.

Rock-Paper-Fire!

Each enemy has a specific element type. The only way the player can defeat them is to switch to the form that is strong against that element type. Fire, Water and Grass are the three element types. Fire beats grass. Grass beats water. Water beats fire.
