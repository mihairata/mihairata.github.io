Marble Mountain

Marble Mountain is the first game I ever created and is a third person game where you control a marble trying to get to the top of a structure. You face increasingly difficult obstacles on your way to the top. I also include a few collectibles that you can look for as well as a power up that helps you reach the end.

Objective

The objective of Marble Mountain is simple> ALl you need to do is make it to the top. Increasingly difficult obstacles stand in your way. These include holes to fall through, rotating platforms and objects that try to push you off.
