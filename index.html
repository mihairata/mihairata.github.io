What is Warrior Brawl?

Warrior Brawl is a two player brawler inspired by Smash Bros. Each character will have their own unique attacks and fighting syle. The combat will be heavily analog giving the player a high ammount of control of the character they are playing as. Each attack will be composed of using the attack button and directional input the player uses with the control stick.

Input

Each character will have their own unique attacks. The player determines which attack to perform with a combination of an attack button and a direction input on the controller's stick. These attacks create a specific collision box depending on the orientation of the attack. Each attack will have it's own specific damage ammount as well as a knockback. Most directional attacks will not involve projectile, but there may be exceptions. The characters will also have their own unique special attacks. These may involve projectiles, buffs and other variable traits. Each character will have a set of the following:

  • Aerial Attacks: Attacks performed after the player jumps

  • Forward Aerial
  • Backward Aerial
  • Upward Aerial
  • Downward Aerial
  • Neutral Aerial (No Directional Input)

  • Grounded Attacks: Attacks performed while player is on ground

  • Standard Attack -- Multiple States* Jab01 >>> Jab02
  • Upward Attack
  • Forward Attack
  • Downward Attack

  • Special Attacks

  • Standard Attack
  • Upward Attack
  • Sideways Attack
  • Downward Attack

Damage

The goal of the game is to rack up as much damage as you can on your opponent and then knocking them off the stage. The more damage a player has the farther they will get sent from opponent attacks. Some attacks will have strong knockback while others will have light knockback. Allowing the players to combo and string attacks together.

'

Objective

If the opponent hits the outer kill zones they lose a life. Every level will have it's own unique kill zone. Some stages will be symmertrical and others will be assymetric. Death zones cane have varying distances from the platfors. Each player will have four lives to start with. After a player loses all their lives, the game is over.
