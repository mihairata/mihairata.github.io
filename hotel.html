Donald Trump's Hotel Defense

Donald Trump's Hotel Defense is my first ever attempt at a game jam. It is a 2D tower defense styled game made in 3 days. The themes for the game jam were "Not a hero" and "Last Resort". I took both of them pretty literally. You play as Donald Trump defending his last remaining hotel with the only weapon he has available: his own head. You endlessly throw your own head at invading tourist zombes. Don't worry, your head always rolls back.
